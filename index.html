About me

As of January 2019, I work full time as a machine learning researcher in the Curious AI company.

I finished my doctoral studies in May 2019 under supervision of Professor Aki Vehtari in the Probabilistic Machine Learning group at Aalto University. My thesis deals with Bayesian statistical inference and machine learning, with the main focus on predictive inference and feature selection for scarce but high-dimensional data using generalized linear models and Gaussian processes.
